About Us

Youth-led. Community driven.

Pathways to Wellness is a youth-led nonprofit based in Fort Worth, Texas. We started this organization because we believe wellness should not be a privilege it should be accessible to every single person in our community, no matter their circumstances.

Who we are

We're not a big organization with fancy offices. We're young people from Fort Worth who care about our neighbors and we're building something real.

Why quality food matters
Lower-income families often have access only to cheap, processed food not because they don't want better, but because quality food is expensive. Our donations go directly toward buying fresh, nutritious groceries and meals for those who otherwise wouldn't have access. That's our commitment.

Our Story

How it all started.

When writing a research paper about ultra-processed foods in my English class, I found that many people, especially in the U.S., consume foods that can cause long-term health conditions like heart disease and obesity.

I knew that not everyone chose to live like that, but that some simply couldn't or didn't know how to live healthier. I wanted to find a way to reach these people and help them.

That's when someone suggested that I start a nonprofit, and I soon got to work to found Pathways to Wellness!
